Our Mission

  • Time Saving

    The D Check requires two months of downtime and 50,000 man-hours. Our innovation dramatically reduces inspection times by automating the checks. With AI-driven damage detection, we significantly minimize aircraft downtime.

  • Accuracy

    The AI system utilizes Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) and You Only Look Once (YOLO) models. This technology ensures precise, real-time damage detection, improving inspection accuracy. Reduces human error, providing more reliable and consistent results.

  • Cost Reduction

    The D Check costs millions in revenue loss. Our solution lowers costs by reducing the man-hours and time required for inspections. Automates checks and improves maintenance processes, saving airlines significant funds annually.

  • Safety

    Enhances safety by providing precise, real-time damage detection. Addresses safety risks by reducing the time between inspections and improving detection accuracy.
